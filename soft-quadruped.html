Design, Sensing and Control of a Tactile Soft Quadruped Robot

Overview

Design, sensing and control of a pneumatically actuated soft quadruped robot with tactile suction cup feet. The work combined soft material design, embedded sensing and locomotion control experiments.

Summary

This MSc project explored soft robotics for legged locomotion. I designed the pneumatic actuation layout, integrated tactile sensors in the feet, and implemented a baseline controller for stable walking. Results include a working prototype capable of planned gaits and basic obstacle negotiation.

  • Role: Thesis author — mechanical design, sensing, control
  • Tools: Python control scripts, pneumatic components, soft-molding
  • Paper & datasets: available on request
