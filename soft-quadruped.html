Design, Sensing and Control of a Tactile Soft Quadruped Robot

Overview

Design, sensing and control of a pneumatically actuated soft quadruped robot with tactile suction cup feet. The work combined soft material design, embedded sensing and locomotion control experiments.

Summary

This MSc project explored soft robotics for legged locomotion. I designed the pneumatic actuation layout, integrated tactile sensors in the feet, implemented a baseline controller for stable walking and improved locomotion performance on flat and inclined terrains using a behavior cloning and reinforcement learning framework.

  • Role: Thesis author — mechanical design, sensing, control
  • Tools: Solidworks, Python, C++, Comsol, pneumatic components, Polyjet and FDM 3d-printing, Silicon molding
  • Paper: available on request
